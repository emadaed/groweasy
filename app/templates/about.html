About GrowEasy Invoice

🇵🇰 Our Mission

Empowering small businesses around the globe and specifically Pakistani small businesses and NGOs with free, tax and audit-compliant inventory, invoicing,CRM all in one.

We believe every business, shopkeeper, freelancer, and startup deserves professional tools without the high cost barrier to ease their operations.

🎯 The Problem We Solve

82% of Pakistani SMBs struggle with:

  • ❌ Expensive accounting and inventory software (5,000-50,000/year)
  • ❌ Complex Tax and Audit compliance requirements
  • ❌ Manual inventory tracking through multiple platforms
  • ❌ Inefficient financial visibility and product management
  • ❌ Overselling, Stockouts, and Order Cancellations
  • ❌ Lack of real-time analytics, Invite your team, Assistant, Accountant
  • ❌ No digital record-keeping for customer, supplier or expense records
